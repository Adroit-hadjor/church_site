Chess Strategies Practice

How to Practice

This simple app provides a starting position for a chessboard. Use it to practice various strategies by moving pieces around. Focus on one strategy at a time:

  • Openings – Practice common openings like the Ruy Lopez or Sicilian Defense.
  • Tactics – Work on forks, pins, skewers and discovered attacks.
  • Endgames – Practice king and pawn endings, rook endings and more.
  • Middlegame Plans – Learn typical plans based on pawn structures and piece activity.

Play out positions repeatedly. Analyze your games afterwards to identify mistakes and improvements. Consistent deliberate practice helps you improve quickly.
